Our digital wallet integration allows you to transfer funds directly from ATM machines to your mobile wallet instantly. Simply select your wallet type, enter the amount, and complete the transfer.

  • Step 1: Insert your card at the ATM and authenticate
  • Step 2: Select "Digital Wallet Transfer" option

  • Step 4: Enter transfer amount and confirm
  • Step 5: Funds appear in your mobile wallet instantly
